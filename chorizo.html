Mexican Chorizo Recipe

Homemade Mexican Chorizo

This homemade Mexican chorizo recipe is easy to make for spicy chorizo sausage with lots of bold chili flavor, great for pork or beef chorizo.

Ingredients

  • Ground pork
  • Paprika
  • Cayenne
  • Ancho powder
  • Guajillo powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Cumin
  • Oregano
  • Coriander
  • Mexican cinnamon
  • Ground clove
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Add ground pork to a large bowl with all of the seasonings and vinegar.
  2. Hand mix the ingredients into the meat until uniformly mixed.
  3. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over a skillet. Add chorizo and cook, breaking apart with a wooden spoon for 8-10 minutes.
  4. Garnish and serve. Perfect for making chorizo tacos, burritos, Mexican tortas, adding to soups, stews, stuffed pepper,and just about any of your favorite spicy foods.
