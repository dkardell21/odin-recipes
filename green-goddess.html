Green Goddess Salad

Simple Greek Goddess Salad uses green cabbage, cucumbers, and a simple homemade Green Goddess salad derssing. Easy cabbage salad reciple!

Ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • Cucumbers
  • Green onions
  • Dressing

Steps

  1. Add ingredients into a blender or food processor.
  2. Blend/mix well combined without any chunks of veggies leftover
  3. The above two steps make the Green Goddess dessing. Next, we assemble the salad.
  4. Add green cabbage, chopped cucumbers, and chopped green onions to a bowl.
  5. Pour the sakad dressing on top.
  6. Use tongs and toss/mix together until coated well.
