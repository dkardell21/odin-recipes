Best Lasagna Ever

Those nights when you're lacking in time and motivation, pull out this no-fail recipe. It'll save the day--or night. This recipe has everything you expect in a classic lasagna: ground beef, tomatoes, noodles,and a delicious selection of cheeses.

Ingredients

  • Italian sausage
  • Lean ground beef
  • One onion
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • One can of crushed tomatoes
  • Two cans tomato sauce
  • Two cans tomato paste
  • Two tlbs white sugar
  • Fresh parsley
  • Dried basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Fennel seeds
  • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesage, mozarella, and ricotta cheese
  • One egg

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.
  3. Stir in tomato products and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and peeper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  4. Bring large pot of slightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodeles, and rinse with cold water.
  5. In mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remainin 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  6. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  7. To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat suace in the bottom of a 9x14-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 Parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to preevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
  9. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  10. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
